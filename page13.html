Out of her little window,

a foreign land she saw.

Empty like a blank slate,

ready for her to draw.

With no one in clear sight

and a pencil close at hand,

she started making doodles

the dawn of her own dream land.

A no man's land no longer,

her characters awoke.

Feeling finished with this world,

she left in a trail of smoke.
